Premium Quality Acrylic Matt

Preparation

Stir Well. All surfaces must be dry and free from grease and loose particles. Soft distemper must be washed off with water or lightly sanded. On porous surfaces ATHOL Acrylic Matt first coat may be thinned up to 10%.

Health & Safety

Keep out of reach of children. In case of contact with eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water. Remove splashes from skin with soap and water or a recognised hand cleaner. Ensure adequate ventilation during application and drying periods. LEAD FREE
